FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What are the admission requirements?

Admission requirements include completed application form, high school transcript of records, certificate of good moral character, birth certificate, and entrance exam results. Please contact our admissions office for more details.

What programs do you offer?

We offer various undergraduate programs including Bachelor of Science in Business Administration (BSBA), Bachelor of Science in Accountancy (BSA), Bachelor of Secondary Education (BSE), Bachelor of Elementary Education (BEE), Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT), and Bachelor of Science in Criminology.

How can I apply for scholarships?

Scholarship applications are available at the start of each academic year. Requirements include academic excellence, financial need assessment, and an interview. Please visit our financial aid office for more information.
